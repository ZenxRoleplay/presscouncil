Critical Rejection Risk Summary

The following issues are the most common reasons for ISSN rejection in India. Ensure every item is resolved before submitting your application reply:

  1. Website title does not match application title exactly.
  2. Publisher address on website differs from address on application form.
  3. Personal name (not institutional name) listed as owner or publishing body.
  4. Gmail / Yahoo used as editorial contact email instead of institutional domain.
  5. First issue not yet published, or contains fewer than 5 articles.
  6. Editorial board has fewer than 5 members, OR members lack institutional affiliations/addresses.
  7. Journal website requires login to access articles or editorial board.
  8. Journal information block (Year, Frequency, Format, Subject, Language) missing from homepage.
